Dr. Preeti Voditel
Head of Department , School of Computer Science Engineering and Emerging Technology

Dr. Preeti Voditel completed her B.E from Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli, Maharashtra. She completed her M.Tech in Computer Science and Information Technology from IIT Kharagpur, West Bengal, and PhD from VNIT, Nagpur, in Computer Science and Engineering. She has several International Research papers to her credit along with patents. She has a work experience of over 22 years out of which she has worked in the industry for 2 years. She has been the Professor Incharge of the ERP at the Institute for ten years. Her research interests include Algorithms, Data Warehousing and Mining, Operating Systems and Affective Computing.
